Our Story

More than decoration
transformation

Lumid INEX is a Lagos-based interior and exterior finishing firm built on the rare combination of structural engineering expertise and design intuition. Founded in 2023, we deliver bespoke residential, commercial and investment-grade fit-outs across Lagos and beyond.

Our approach is precision-first every material, finish and layout decision is made with both aesthetic impact and long-term asset performance in mind. When Lumid touches a space, we don't just decorate, we rethink how it feels, functions, and performs as an asset.

Led by a first-class civil engineer with hands-on site experience across Lagos, our team brings structural understanding, design intuition and project management discipline to every engagement. Clients range from homeowners and Airbnb hosts to property developers and real estate investors.
