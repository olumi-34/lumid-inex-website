Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Lumid Interior & Exterior Finishes ("Lumid", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, why, and how it's handled, in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit through our consultation form or checkout: name, phone number, email, delivery address
  • Project details you share with us: budget range, service interest, description of your space
  • Order information when you purchase from the Lumid Store: items purchased, payment reference (processed by Paystack — we do not store your card details)

How We Use It

  • To respond to consultation requests and follow up via WhatsApp, phone, or email
  • To process and fulfil Lumid Store orders and deliveries
  • To share project progress updates if you're an active client

How We Store It

Information submitted through this site is stored securely via Supabase, our database provider. Payments are processed directly by Paystack; we never see or store your card details.

Sharing

We do not sell your information. We only share it with service providers directly involved in fulfilling your request (e.g. Paystack for payment processing, delivery partners for order fulfilment).

Your Rights

You can request to see, correct, or delete the information we hold about you at any time by contacting us at WhatsApp or by phone.
